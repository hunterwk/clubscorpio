About me:

Miguel “Avocado” Ortiz was born and raised in San Diego. At a young age he learned to draw in order to create his own comic book characters. His interest in art grew exponentially when he discovered Lowrider Arte, Comics, and Tattoo Magazine. He began tattooing in 2013 but didn’t pursue it seriously until 2016, and in November of 2019 began tattooing out of the Lower Left in East Village, a neighborhood in downtown San Diego. His tattoo style knows no bounds but he specialized in small/medium, bold, minimalist, traditional black and grey, and cover up tattoos.
